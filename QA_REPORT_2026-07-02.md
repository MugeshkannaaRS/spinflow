## Findings (non-blocking cleanup / tech-debt)

### 1. Vestigial SaaS endpoints called by orphan pages — *low priority*
These frontend calls have **no backend endpoint** (single-mill dropped the feature), but they only sit on pages **not present in the sidebar** (reachable only by typing the URL), and the frontend degrades gracefully (try/catch → null):

- `/admin/billing/*`, `/subscription/plans`, `/subscription/companies` — multi-tenant billing/subscriptions
- `/customer/help/*`, `/customer/onboarding/*`, `/customer/engagement` — customer-success/help-center
- `/nudges`, `/tours`, `/auth/register` — growth/self-serve features

**Orphan pages** (0 sidebar references — same category as the routes we just deleted): `help-center`, `onboarding`, `executive-dashboard`, `import-hub`, `archive`, plus the billing/subscription admin analytics/alerts pages. Candidates for removal, or wiring up if you actually want them.

Also dead: `billingApi` / subscription helpers still shipped in `src/lib/api-service.ts`.

### 2. FastAPI deprecation warnings — *cosmetic*
`Query(..., regex=...)` is deprecated in favour of `pattern=`:
- `backend/app/api/v1/audit.py:250`
- `backend/app/api/v1/masters.py:772`

Plus `pythonjsonlogger.jsonlogger` import moved to `pythonjsonlogger.json`. Harmless today; will break on a future major bump.

### 3. Confirmed matcher false-positives (no action)
`attachments`, `payroll/employees`, `packing/last-bag`, `qr/history`, `stock/lot`, `notifications/read-all`, `admin/companies/:id/modules`, `admin/mills/:id/settings` all resolve to real, parameterised backend routes.
